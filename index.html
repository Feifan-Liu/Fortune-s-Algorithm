Instruction:
1. Click on board or use "Generate random points" to add points;
2. After finishing adding points, click on "Start" first to initialize the algorithm;
3. "Step" : run Fortune's algorithm step by step;
4. "Finish" : complete all remaining steps;
5. "Reset": remove all objects on canvas;
6. "Reset Line Position": reset sweep line position to the middle;
7. "Show/Hide parabola": switch between showing beach line only or showing beach line and all parabolas (default: hide);
8. "Animation: ON/OFF": turn off/on animation of each step;
9. You can move the sweep line by dragging the red point on it (will not effect the algorithm execution);
10. If running slow, please try starting with less points, clicking "Hide parabola" or move mouse in and out the canvas;
11. Please do not add points after clicking "Start";
12. Green lines on the board shows the positions of current vertex events.
